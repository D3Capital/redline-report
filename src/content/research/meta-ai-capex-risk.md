# Meta AI Capex Risk

Meta is being pulled into an AI capital expenditure cycle that may pressure free cash flow more severely than the market currently discounts.

This is a preliminary research note. The core thesis is that the market may be treating AI capex as optional growth investment, while the competitive structure of large-scale AI may make it increasingly mandatory.

## Core thesis

Meta's AI spending could reduce future capital return flexibility if revenue growth does not accelerate enough to offset higher infrastructure investment.

## What would disprove this thesis

The thesis would be weakened if Meta sustains strong advertising growth, keeps capex growth under control, and protects free cash flow margins while continuing large buybacks.****
